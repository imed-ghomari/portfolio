Designed a personal media workflow where playback progress, consumption state, and ordering are stored in structured JSON.

### Technical Overview:
- **Syncing**: Synced media files and metadata across devices using **Syncthing**.
- **Playback**: Explored interoperability with existing Android players vs. building a custom player.
- **Evaluation**: Evaluated ExoPlayer, Video.js, PyVidPlayer2, and Kivy-based Android packaging with performance and format-support trade-offs.
