Quran Life is a memorization-focused web app designed for **Quran hifdh**. Its core concept combines **visual mindmaps** with **spaced repetition** to help learners retain verses more effectively over time.

**[Visit Website](https://quran-life.org/)**

### Why I built it
- Make memorization more structured and less overwhelming.
- Turn long-term revision into a clear, repeatable routine.
- Use visual organization to strengthen understanding and recall.

### Core approach
- **Visual Mindmaps** to map relationships between surahs and ayat.
- **Spaced Repetition** cycles to review at the right time for stronger retention.
- **Hifdh-first design** focused on consistent daily progress.
