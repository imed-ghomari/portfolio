A browser extension built around a simple productivity philosophy: each tab group represents a single task or project, making it easier to stay focused and reduce context switching. The keyboard shortcuts are designed to support that workflow by letting the user manage tab groups quickly without breaking concentration.

**[View on GitHub](https://github.com/imed-ghomari/tab-shortcuts-extension)**

### Highlights:
- **Task-based tab grouping** so each group can hold the tabs for one project or area of focus.
- **Keyboard-driven group management** for faster control without interrupting flow.
- **Focus-oriented workflow design** aimed at reducing context switching during deep work.
